Events Team

The Events department organizes all of CVEC’s events including our Insight Series and Pitch Day Series. The Events department's roles include connecting with guest speakers and startup projects, planning event themes, and hosting and representing CVEC in our events. In the past, we’ve connected with 50+ top VC firms across continents with positive feedback and continuous support.

Marketing Team

The Marketing Department takes charge of the public representation of CVEC across social media platforms and our broader community across the world. The Marketing department’s roles include composing and designing all marketing materials from posters to newsletters, communicating activities within the club and with partnering organizations, and managing official platforms from our website, Instagram, Linkedin, email newsletters, to Wechat articles, group chats, and our representative account.
